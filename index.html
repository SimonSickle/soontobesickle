Our Story

The Ninth of June

We met eachother in high school in a dual enrollment American History class. It wasn't love at first site, though. I asked Hannah out three times before she finally said yes! Once I finally got the date and decided to make things offical, she almost said no. If it wasn't for a random person stepping on her hand in the stadium, we might not be here today. I've always known that I got lucky meeting her, but I definately had someone up there on my side when it comes to us. Now I can't imagine a day without her and I can't wait to spend the rest of my life together.

The Day They Got Engaged

It was a hot, muggy day at Disney's Animal Kingdom. We rode a train through Everest and visited the na'vi in Pandora; you know, the normal stuff. As the day went on, I was secretly getting more and more nervous, though. I'd like to think she wasn't expecting me to pop the question, but there is no way I wasn't showing how nervous I was.

After we called it quits exploring Pandora, we headed to the Tree of Life to see the projection show. Once the show was over, I convinced Hannah that we should get a picture in front of the tree by the photographer Disney had posted. As we were walking up I set our bags down and showed the photographer a quick glance at a ring box. I planned a big long speach out, but I think I barely got the words "Will you marry me?" out.

Of course, the rest is history; that's why you are here afterall. We're looking forward to sharing our day with with you! See you on June 9th!
